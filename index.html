Jordon's Coffee Corner

Welcome to Jordon's Coffee Corner! Explore the menu options below

Double Espresso

A strong and intense shot of espresso, perfect for those who need a quick and powerful caffeine boost.

Cortado

Equal parts espresso and steamed milk, creating a smooth and balanced drink with a thin layer of foam.

Cappuccino

A classic espresso drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, offering a balance of coffee flavor and creamy texture.

Latte

Made with espresso and steamed milk, with a small amount of milk foam on top. Lattes offer a milder coffee flavor than straight espresso.

Iced Latte

A refreshing latte served over ice, perfect for a hot day.

Americano

Espresso diluted with hot water, creating a lighter and more tea-like coffee drink compared to straight espresso.

Americano w/ Cold Foam

A twist on the classic Americano, topped with a layer of cold, frothed milk for a creamy texture.

Espresso Tonic

A bubbly and refreshing drink made with espresso, tonic water, and ice.

Affogato

A delightful dessert coffee made with a scoop of vanilla ice cream topped with a shot of hot espresso.

Brown Sugar Latte w/ Olive Oil

A unique latte variation that combines the sweetness of brown sugar with the savory richness of olive oil, creating a complex and flavorful drink.

Steamer

Hot, steamed milk flavored with your choice of syrups or powders. A perfect option for those who enjoy a milky coffee drink without the strong coffee flavor.

S'mores Latte

A decadent latte flavored with chocolate and marshmallow syrups, topped with whipped cream and graham cracker crumbs, for a taste of the classic campfire treat.
