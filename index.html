Welcome to Jordon's Coffee Corner! Explore the menu options below

Double Espresso

A strong and intense shot of espresso, perfect for those who need a quick and powerful caffeine boost.

Cortado

Equal parts espresso and steamed milk, creating a smooth and balanced drink with a thin layer of foam.

Cappuccino

A classic espresso drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, offering a balance of coffee flavor and creamy texture.

Latte

Made with espresso and steamed milk, with a small amount of milk foam on top. Lattes offer a milder coffee flavor than straight espresso.
